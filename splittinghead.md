# Rabi Oscillations

\[
\begin{bmatrix}
| \Psi_r \rangle  \\
| \Psi_l \rangle
\end{bmatrix}
= \begin{bmatrix}
E_0 & ϵ \\
ϵ & E_0 \\
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
| \Psi_r \rangle \\
| \Psi_l \rangle
\end{bmatrix}
\]

\[
\lambda^2 - 2 E_0 \lambda + E_0^2 + \epsilon^2 = 0
\]

\[
\lambda = E_0\pm \sqrt{E_0^2-  \left(E_0^2 + \epsilon^2 \right)}
\]
\[
\lambda = E_0 \pm i \epsilon
\]
